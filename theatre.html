DESCRIPTION

Theatrical Culture is Another Face of Art Which has Been Enriching Indian Culture From 15th Century BC. In the Recent Days of Modernity And Cultural Division,Theatre is Becoming the Corner Stone. "Pratibimba Theatre Group" ,Created by "Pragatishil CGECian", is a Small Attempt to Recover Our Theatrical World from the Extinction and to Put a Smile on the Faces of Theatre-Lovers.
